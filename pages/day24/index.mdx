## What you'll learn here

- **Fixed-size windows** — max sum of size k, average of last n elements, anagrams
- **Variable-size windows** — longest substring without repeating, minimum window substring
- The **expand/contract template** that solves most of them in ~10 lines
- Common pitfalls: forgetting to update the answer when contracting, off-by-one with right pointer

You already met sliding window in [Sliding Window Maximum](/day4/queues/practice_questions/sliding_window_maximum) and [Number of Recent Calls](/day4/queues/practice_questions/recent_calls). This chapter formalizes the pattern.
