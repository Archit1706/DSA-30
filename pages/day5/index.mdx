# Day 5

### Hello peeps! Welcome to Day 5 - Recursion

> What we learn today?

-   Understand the concept of recursion and its importance in solving complex problems.

-   Study the working principle of recursive functions and how they call themselves.
-   Learn about the base case and recursive case in recursive functions.
-   Understand the process of recursion using the call stack.
-   Explore examples of recursion, such as factorial, Fibonacci sequence, and recursive tree traversal algorithms.
-   Analyze the time and space complexity of recursive algorithms.
